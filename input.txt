The stanford gives advance education.The Leland Stanford Junior University, commonly referred
 to as Stanford University or Stanford, is an American private research university located in
 Stanford, California, on an 8,180-acre (3,310 ha) campus near Palo Alto. It is situated in 
the northwestern Silicon Valley, approximately 20 miles (32 km) northwest of San Jose and 
37 miles (60 km) southeast of San Francisco.Stanford is considered to be one of the most 
prestigious universities in the world.Stanford was founded by Leland Stanford, a railroad
 magnate, United States Senator, and former California governor, and his wife, Jane Stanford.
 It is named in honor of their only child, Leland Stanford, Jr., who died in 1884 just before
 his 16th birthday. His parents decided to dedicate a university to their only son, and Leland
 Stanford told his wife, "The children of California shall be our children." The Stanfords 
visited Harvard's president, Charles Eliot, and asked how much it would cost to duplicate Harvard
 in California. Eliot replied that he supposed $15 million (in 1884 dollars) would be enough.
